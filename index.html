Transforming Our World: The 2030 Agenda

The Sustainable Development Goals are a universal call to action to end poverty, protect the planet, and ensure that all people enjoy peace and prosperity by 2030.

Explore the Goals

Our Focus: Sustainable Development Goals 1–5

These five goals form the foundation for sustainable development, addressing the most fundamental human needs and rights.

Goal 1: No Poverty

End poverty in all its forms everywhere

  • Eradicate extreme poverty
  • Reduce poverty by at least 50%
  • Implement social protection systems
Learn More

Goal 2: Zero Hunger

End hunger, achieve food security and improved nutrition

  • End malnutrition
  • Double agricultural productivity
  • Ensure sustainable food production systems
Learn More

Goal 3: Good Health

Ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all

  • Reduce maternal mortality
  • End preventable deaths of newborns
  • Combat communicable diseases
Learn More

Goal 4: Quality Education

Ensure inclusive and equitable quality education

  • Free primary and secondary education
  • Equal access to technical education
  • Increase number of qualified teachers
Learn More

Goal 5: Gender Equality

Achieve gender equality and empower all women and girls

  • End discrimination against women
  • Eliminate violence against women
  • Ensure women's full participation in leadership
Learn More
